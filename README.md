# Remote CX Job Sources

A curated list of career pages worth monitoring.  for remote Customer Experience, Customer Success, Operations, and Support leadership roles.

## What's in here

`discovered_sources.json` — a list of company career pages, each with:
- `name` — company name
- `url` — direct link to their careers page (often pre-filtered for relevant roles)
- `type` — source type (e.g. `career_page`)
- `source_list` — where the company was originally discovered

## Who this is for

Job seekers looking for roles at top company who want to monitor company career pages directly rather than relying solely on LinkedIn or checking individual job boards manually.

## How to use it

- Use this list with a scraper
- Browse the JSON and visit pages manually
- Use it as a seed list for a job monitoring script
- Fork it and add your own sources

## Contributing

Found a company worth adding? Open a PR with the new entry following the existing format.

## Notes

- YMMV - Your Mileage May Vary with this list as companies often change their hiring platforms or page structures change. 
- URLs are often pre-filtered (e.g. by department or remote location) — double-check filters if a page looks empty
- Some pages change their URL structure over time
